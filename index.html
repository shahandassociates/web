About Us

Shah & Associates is located in Kolkata and Howrah and specializes in Corporate, Criminal, and Civil matters before the High Court, NCLT, NCLAT, DRT, DRAT, Consumer Forums, and lower courts.

The firm has a presence across India including Delhi, Ranchi, Uttar Pradesh, and Patna, representing corporate clients nationwide while providing a unique blend of litigation and advisory services.

Practice Areas

Corporate Law +

Our firm's proficiency in corporate litigation encompasses representing clients before various legal bodies, including NCLT, NCLAT, DRT, and DRAT, in cases related to Insolvency & Bankruptcy, Oppression & Mismanagement, Arbitration & Conciliation, and Takeover, Merger & Amalgamation. Our expertise extends to providing comprehensive legal support and representation in a wide range of corporate legal matters, ensuring the effective protection of our clients' interests and rights.

IPO, FPO & Rights Issue +

Our law firm, Shah & Associates, serves as Legal Advisors for IPO, FPO, and Rights Issue of Shares and Debentures. We are experienced in conducting legal due diligence and drafting prospectuses in collaboration with merchant bankers to ensure adherence to BSE and National Stock Exchange regulations. Additionally, we possess expertise in the process of listing and delisting shares on Stock Exchanges, providing comprehensive support in navigating the complexities of these procedures.

Criminal Matters +

Our firm offers legal services that include providing advice and representation in criminal cases. Our associates handle cases under various acts such as the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988, Domestic Violence Act, 2005, Dowry Prohibition Act 1961, and economic offences of cheating, criminal breach of trust, forgery, and matrimonial disputes. We provide representation from trial courts to the High Court, including Bail and Anticipatory Bail applications and applications in exercise of the inherent powers of the High Court within its revisory jurisdiction..

Family Law +

Our firm specializes in Matrimonial and Family laws, offering comprehensive advisory and representation services to both domestic and foreign clients. We actively represent clients in various legal forums, including family courts, district courts, High Courts in India, the Supreme Court, National Human Rights Commission, YWCA, National Commission of Women, and other Women’s cell/organizations. Our services cover a wide range of areas, including but not limited to:

  • Divorce
  • Registration of Marriages
  • Dowry Demand Complaint Cases
  • Custody
  • Alimony Cases
  • Judicial Separation
  • Domestic Violence
  • Police Protection
  • Restitution of Conjugal Rights
  • Maintenance
  • Adoption & Guardianship
  • Adoption
  • Muslim Laws
  • Custody of Child/Children
  • Inheritance & Succession
  • Probate & Wills
Property Law +

Our law firm, Shah & Associates, offers a wide range of services related to property law. Our team of experienced professionals is well-versed in various legal matters and can assist in safeguarding your rights, whether you are looking to purchase or lease property, defend your rights in court, or require guidance on estate matters. We provide counsel on the acquisition, development, and leasing of real estate, with extensive experience in representing buyers, sellers, lenders, developers, and landlords in the negotiation and preparation of various real estate agreements. Additionally, we provide advisory services on zoning, land use, title and boundary disputes, condemnation, and other related matters. Our firm also offers expertise in assisting clients with property transfer, registration of sale deeds, mortgage, gift, exchange, and lease of immovable property.

Intellectual Property Rights +

Shah & Associates is a legal firm that provides a range of advisory, assistance, and dispute resolution services related to intellectual property rights. Our team of professionals is experienced in drafting various agreements related to the assignment and licensing of intellectual property rights, providing advisory services on different transactional work involving IP rights, and conducting search and intelligence services, including due diligence, investigation, market surveys, negotiations for co-existence agreements and buy-outs. We also offer services related to drafting, filing, prosecution, and opposition of applications of trademarks, patents, copyrights, geographical indications, business processes, and the filing of PCT applications for patents. Our firm represents clients before various authorities such as the Trade Mark Registry, Copyright Office, Geographical Indications Registry, National Informatics Centre, and Intellectual Property Appellate Board (IPAB), along with drafting technology transfer and licensing compliances, estimating the intellectual property of our clients, and more.

Dispute Resolution & Arbitration +

Shah & Associates is a highly experienced firm that handles a wide range of Civil, Criminal, and Corporate litigations. The firm's Litigation & Arbitration team is composed of Advocates-on-Record, competent paralegal staff, and Advocates. In addition to traditional litigation, the firm is dedicated to safeguarding the interests of its clients and resolving commercial disputes between private parties through alternative methods such as Domestic Arbitration, Mediation, and Reconciliation. This approach reflects the firm's commitment to providing effective and efficient dispute resolution services, ensuring that clients' needs are met across various legal domains..

Consumer Disputes +

Shah & Associates is a legal firm that provides advisory and litigation services for consumer complaints, disputes, and complaints related to monopolistic and restrictive trade practices. Our team of experienced professionals has a wealth of knowledge in this area and can act quickly to seek appropriate orders, from the District Forum to the National Commission. We offer comprehensive legal support and representation to safeguard our clients' rights, whether they are consumers seeking redressal or businesses facing consumer complaints. Additionally, we provide guidance on matters related to estate planning, property law, and corporate law, among others.

Our philosophy

We are committed to providing our clients with the most effective legal remedies, with a specific emphasis on preserving valuable time.

As specialists in litigation, we are dedicated to resolving cases prior to the commencement of hearings. Through our expertise in drafting petitions, we concentrate on the essential facts of each case and clearly outline the legal solution, thereby averting unnecessary arguments and delays before the adjudicating authority.
